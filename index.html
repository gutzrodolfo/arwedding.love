Juchipila, Zac.

Our wedding will take place in beautiful Juchipila, Zacatecas. Gently tucked between emerald mountains lies a hidden gem known as el Cañón de Juchipila. The smell of churros, sweet sugar cane, and sounds of laughter flow through the alleyways, as the hums of the town's church bells echo amongst the streets. Overlooked by the Sierra De Santa Cruz and glow of the moon, Juchipila maintains its charm by way of its residents. Join us January 3rd as we invite you to celebrate our marriage alongside the cañon's breeze, friends, and family.
